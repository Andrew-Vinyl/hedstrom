# Hedstrom Google Analytics 4 Insights: Q4 2025

**Date Ranges Analysed:**

- Current Quarter: Oct 1, 2025 – Dec 31, 2025 (Q4 2025)
- Previous Quarter: Jul 1, 2025 – Sep 30, 2025 (Q3 2025)
- Prior Year Quarter: Oct 1, 2024 – Dec 31, 2024 (Q4 2024)

## Key Metrics & Performance Trends

### 1. Significant YoY Traffic Growth With Lower Depth

- **Sessions:** 8,739 in Q4 2025 vs 6,727 in Q4 2024 (**+29.9%**)
- **Active Users:** 7,056 in Q4 2025 vs 5,425 in Q4 2024 (**+30.1%**)
- **Pageviews:** 19,614 in Q4 2025 vs 22,494 in Q4 2024 (**-12.8%**)

*Insight:* While Hedstrom is successfully driving about 30% more users to the site than last year, users are viewing fewer pages. The homepage specifically saw a steep drop in raw page views YoY (from 10,074 down to 5,478), despite a slight increase in homepage sessions. This implies users are either finding what they need instantly, or they are bouncing without clicking deeper into the site.

### 2. Channel Performance is Highly Concentrated

- **Organic Search (Q4 2025):** 4,499 sessions (51% of total traffic)
- **Direct (Q4 2025):** 3,957 sessions (45% of total traffic)
- **Other Channels:** Organic Social contributed just 130 sessions, and Referral contributed 103 sessions.

*Insight:* SEO and brand awareness are extremely strong and are driving 96% of the traffic. However, this is a risky single-point dependency. There is considerable untapped potential in optimizing traffic from Paid Search, Organic Social, or Referral partnerships.

### 3. "Retailers" Page is Gaining Traction

- **Q4 2025:** 1,875 pageviews (1,590 sessions)
- **Q3 2025:** 1,647 pageviews (1,434 sessions)
- **Q4 2024:** 1,550 pageviews (1,302 sessions)

*Insight:* Unlike overall page views which trended downward, the `/retailers/` page actually increased QoQ and YoY. This signals a growing interest—either from B2B partners or from consumers looking for physical store locations to purchase Hedstrom products.

### 4. Top Performing Product Lines Show Clear Favorites

The top three specific category pages by page views in Q4 2025 were:

1. `/products/` (3,139 views)
2. `/playballs/` (2,158 views)
3. `/foam/` (270 views)
4. `/hoppers/` (253 views)

*Insight:* "Playballs" is consistently the runaway favorite specific product category, maintaining strong viewership numbers over 2,100 each quarter. "Foam" and "Hoppers" also have dedicated bases.

### 5. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) & Schema Audit

We crawled through Hedstrom.com's product pages to find a significant gap in AEO optimization:

1. **Lack of Product Schema Markup**
   Currently, none of the checked product pages feature structured `Product` schema markup (JSON-LD). This means Answer Engines (like ChatGPT or Google's AI Overviews) have to guess the page's purpose rather than being fed explicitly structured and standardized details (like price, availability, brand, or SKU).
2. **Shallow Content Depth**
   The product pages average just under 500 words, and the vast majority of that text comes from boilerplate footer, cookie warning, and navigation menus. The actual product descriptions are typically 3-4 bullet points and roughly 50-70 words long. This is insufficient for AEO, which relies on dense, highly informative context to answer user queries effectively.

#### Why AEO Matters

*(Answer Engine Optimization focuses on structuring your site's content so AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Google's AI summary can easily read, understand, and cite your products directly in their conversational answers.)*

## Strategic Recommendations for Next Quarter (Q1/Q2 2026)

1. **Optimize the Homepage for Engagement (Reduce the Bounce):** Since users are coming in higher volume but venturing less deeply, test adding more prominent, friction-free calls-to-action (CTAs) on the homepage. If `/playballs/` is the clear favorite, consider placing a bold Playballs section "above the fold" to immediately channel the high volume of visitors to your top product.
2. **Capitalize on Retailer Intent:** Since traffic to `/retailers/` is climbing consistently, confirm this page is highly optimized. Maximizing the capture rate on this page can lead to outsized revenue outcomes.
3. **Diversify Traffic Sources:** While Organic and Direct are fantastic, we suggest experimenting with Paid Social (Meta/Instagram ads) or Influencer Referrals targeting the specific demographic interested in playballs and active kids' toys. An additional 10-15% bump from Social could easily push total quarterly traffic over the 10k session threshold.
4. **Content Push for Underperforming Lines:** Products like `/foam/` and `/hoppers/` are drawing only ~10% of the traffic compared to playballs. Consider featuring them in blog posts, holiday/spring buying guides, or targeted social media campaigns to boost discovery.
5. **Strategic Proposal for AEO** We strongly recommend filling out product page details and updating/upgrading the Yoast SEO plugin (or migrating to RankMath SEO's paid plan to get more-robust Schema tools) to instantly deploy structured data site-wide. Along with adding structured data, we should also test expanding product descriptions with FAQs and detailed specifications to provide the dense context that Answer Engines look for when citing sources.
